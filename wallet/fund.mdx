After creating your wallet, you will need to activate it by sending at least **1 XRP** to your wallet.
This will serve as your [base reserve](/wallet/reserves) and is required by the XRP Ledger to fight against spam.

Your wallet dashboard displays your unique wallet address and QR code. You can either:

- Copy your wallet address directly
- Use the QR code to scan and send XRP

## Options to fund your wallet

You can fund your wallet by sending XRP from a centralized exchange, credit/debit card on-ramp service, or transfer from another existing wallet.

### Centralized exchanges

Centralized exchanges are the most popular way to fund your wallet. Here are a few options:

- [Coinbase](https://coinbase.com)
- [Binance](https://binance.com)
- [Kraken](https://kraken.com)
- [Uphold](https://uphold.com)

### Using a credit/debit card

You can use a service that allows you to buy XRP with a credit/debit card and have it delivered directly to your wallet. Here are a couple of options:

- [MoonPay](https://moonpay.com)
- [Ramp](https://ramp.network)
