## How It Works

The Motion DEX leverages the XRP Ledger's built-in exchange, allowing you to:

- Trade issued tokens alongside XRP
- Execute trades with near-instant settlement
- Monitor your trading history
- Access real-time insights

## Trading Security

Our DEX inherits the security and reliability of the XRP Ledger. All trades are executed on-chain, ensuring complete transparency.
